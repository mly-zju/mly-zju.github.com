关于我

Hi, welcome to my blog! My name is Ma Lingyang(马龄阳).

I received my B.S. Degree in Electronic Engineering from Zhejiang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2014. I am currently a master student with the Wireless and Networking Lab at the UM-SJTU Joint Institute, Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

During postgraduate study, my research interests are mainly focused on Wifi and Internet of Things(IoT), including MAC protocol, routing algorithm, and application software.

At present, my career development direction is web development, and I'm about to enter Baidu in Beijing as a front-end developer.

And you're also welcomed to visit my github, where you can see most source codes of my projects introduced in this blog. Thanks!
